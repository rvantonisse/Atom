Darts101

When you need an advanced whiteboard for your darts scoring.

Play darts with your friends and save the games on your smartphone, tablet or pc! If you are a pro, keep track of your statistics and train yourself for coming tournaments.

Currently supporting the following features:

  • Play a 1 player X01 game - a classic darts game with options!

Coming soon

  • Cricket / Tackticks - Fire your darts at 20 to bullseye
  • Training games:
    • Finisher - train your finishing scores, from 1 dart to 3 dart finishes.
    • Accuracy training - throw 3 darts at a given target; singles, doubles & triples.
  • Statistics:
    • X01 Match averages
    • X01 Doubles accuracy
  • Player creation and saving locally, not online!
